NOURISHING THE NATION!

The vision here at Nourish is to change people’s perception of a vending machine. In a busy day it’s often hard to find a healthy snack at your convenience, so we got thinking… and with a passion for providing an exciting alternative to the usual vending JUNK, along came NOURISH!

Our Machines

The machines we provide will add value to any environment we Nourish, and our excellent service will help to form strong relationships with all clients by delivering a successful and sustainable operation.

Our machines are now more energy efficient and environmentally-friendly than ever. Thus, up to 50% on energy savings are possible compared to conventional solutions and all have an A+ energy class rating.

Our Products

We work with some of the leading healthy brands and our versatile machines can stock an array of products such as dried fruits and nuts, seeds, healthy noodles, couscous, coconut waters and natural fruit juices to name but a few!

The contents of each machine will be specifically chosen to suit your location, therefore, each individual product will be carefully scrutinized by our health food expert to ensure only the best prevail and reach our machines.

Charity

We feel strongly about building up relationships with relevant charities and any initiatives that can help deliver our healthy living message. So, we support the government's Change4Life campaign and actively seek to associate ourselves with charitable brands.

As a result all the water we stock comes from Thirsty Planet whose partnership with Pumpaid raises money towards water projects in the most disadvantaged parts of Africa.
